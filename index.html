Subscription troubleshooting

  • Ensure you have the latest app version installed.
  • To restore purchases: open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases.
  • If Subscribe does nothing in TestFlight: sign out of the App Store (Settings → Apple ID → Sign Out), then attempt the purchase and sign in with your Sandbox Tester credentials when prompted.

Testing / reviewer note

Sandbox / TestFlight

Create a Sandbox Tester in App Store Connect and use those credentials when prompted. Dev bypass (ephemeral): enter qwerty12345 on the paywall to unlock the app until restart (for review only).

What to include in a support request

  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 14) and iOS version
  • App build number
  • Short description of the issue

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-27

Forge Fitness ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This page summarizes what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Contact info you provide when you email support.
  • Anonymous usage and analytics to improve the app.
  • Subscription status and transaction identifiers via Apple StoreKit to manage access. Payment details are handled by Apple.

How we use data

We use data to operate and improve the app, manage subscriptions, and respond to support requests.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with third-party services for analytics or crash reporting under their privacy terms, and with Apple for subscription verification.

Your choices

You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings. To request data deletion or ask privacy questions, email bifrostnetworks@gmail.com.

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.
