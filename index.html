Subscription troubleshooting

  1. Ensure you have the latest app version installed.
  2. To restore purchases: open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases.
  3. If Subscribe does nothing in TestFlight: sign out of the App Store (Settings → Apple ID → Sign Out), then attempt the purchase and sign in when prompted with your Sandbox Tester credentials.

Testing / Review notes

Sandbox / TestFlight

For testers: create a Sandbox Tester in App Store Connect and use those credentials when prompted during purchase.

Reviewer dev bypass (ephemeral): enter the code qwerty12345 on the paywall to unlock the app until the next app restart.

What to include in a support request

Please include:

  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 14) and iOS version
  • App build number (from Settings → About)
  • A short description of the issue and any console logs if available

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-27

Forge Fitness ("we", "us", "the App") respects your privacy. This page explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how you can contact us.

Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Account and contact information you provide when you contact support (email).
  • Usage data and analytics about how you use the App (features used, crashes, device type).
  • Purchase and subscription status via Apple’s StoreKit (receipt/transaction identifiers) to manage access. We do not collect full payment details — those are handled by Apple.

How we use information

We use data to: operate and improve the App, manage subscriptions, respond to support requests, and for analytics to improve performance and features.

Sharing & third parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with third‑party services (analytics or crash reporting) under their privacy terms, and with Apple for subscription verification. Third parties process data on our behalf and are required to protect it.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or until you request deletion where applicable.

Your choices

You can opt out of analytics (where supported) and manage subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings. To request data deletion or ask privacy questions, contact us at support@forgefitness.example.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
